title: Documentation Page Not Showing
description: Fix missing documentation pages in Vincent v.B.
---

## Symptoms

- Added page doesn't appear in project
- Page seems missing from sidebar

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Project Structure">
    Ensure your new page is placed in the correct section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh the Browser">
    Press Ctrl+R (Windows) or Cmd+R (Mac) to reload your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-login if needed">
    Log out and back in to sync recent updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>
