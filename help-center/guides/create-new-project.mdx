## Create a Project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    Go to `https://vincentvb.com/dashboard` and sign in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Project">
    Click the "Create Project" button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Add your project title and an optional description. Click "Create".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Pages">
    Click "Add Page" to begin creating documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize Sections">
    Drag and drop pages in the sidebar to arrange sections as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
