<Callout kind="info" title="Prerequisites">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: Google account for quick sign-up
</Callout>

## Sign Up and Log In

Create your Vincent v.B. account in seconds. Choose your preferred method below.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email" icon="mail">
    Visit [https://app.vincentvb.com/signup](https://app.vincentvb.com/signup) and enter your email and password.
    
    Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Google" icon="apple">
    Click the Google button on the sign-up page and authorize Vincent v.B.
    
    No additional verification needed.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
Save your login details securely. Use a password manager for best security.
</Callout>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, follow these steps to set up your project.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard" icon="home">
    Click **New Space** from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`.
    
    Select your brand color (`#3B82F6`) and add a description: "Welcome to Vincent v.B.'s documentation."
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate Starter Pages" icon="file-plus">
    Choose **Quickstart Template** to auto-generate pages like introduction and changelog.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Basic Interface Tour
